Services

  • Linking climate change science with policy and action

    With more than five years of experience working on climate change preparedness from the climate science and local government perspectives, Adaptation International has the expertise and experience to bridge the gap between climate change science and local community action. We are uniquely positioned to help develop climate resilient communities and businesses.

  • Community and business climate preparedness planning

    It can be difficult for businesses and local governments to know where to start when it comes to preparing for climate change. Adaptation International can help by working with clients to bringing in the right stakeholders, create a framework for the development of climate adaptation plans, and coordinate the development and prioritization of adaptation strategies. This type of planning process will allow clients to discuss climate risks, prepare for climate impacts, and build capacity for extreme weather and climate related emergencies.

  • Tools to mainstream adaptation into planning

    Decision-support and planning tools can help get businesses and communities the climate impact information they need, when they need it. Truly responding to the issue of climate change, minimizing risk, and taking advantage of new opportunities will require building consideration of potential climate impacts into all levels of planning and decision-making. Adaptation International helps communities and businesses develop the tools necessary to mainstream consideration of climate change into their everyday actions.

  • Grant applications and funding

    A woman-owned business, Adaptation International makes an excellent partner for local governments and environmental organizations. We have extensive experience evaluating, coordinating, and writing grant applications. Our focus on climate change impacts and adaptation strategies makes us well suited to lead or round out a project proposal team.

  • Client centered personal services

    Climate impacts vary by region as do the needs of individual communities and businesses. Adaptation International works on a project specific basis to provide cost effective services tailored to the individual clients needs.
